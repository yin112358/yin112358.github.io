「约稿」印象诗

原创 / 宇宙灯塔 / 「约稿」印象诗

  • 发表于 2021.04.20

夏天我常在炎热的天空里发呆
I dazed with the glowing summer sky
海水寄居在空中的流云
Water from the ocean dwelled as clouds
只是一行
Maybe they were only lines
留在纸上的文字
Lines written on Someone’s scroll.


如今万人都应翻译作盲目
Faith shall be translated into blindness;
我只追求着一个梦的途径
I tracked down to a path of dream
小心地卧在渺茫的天边
Curling up under the heaven, quietly
屏住呼吸，看人们没有见过的事
Holding my breathe
I saw landscapes they never knew.


纸张还是幕布？
Paper or curtain?
我是标点，抑或演员
I was born as a comma, or maybe actor
我梦想抱住降落的笔尖
When the Nib touch the paper, I would
hold it with both of my arms
地面上的人
People on the ground
只捕捉到天际的一闪
Would only have a glance.


我是生在宇宙的夹缝里
I opened my eyes between pages
（不，当然不是书签；我没有那样伟大）
(No, definitely not a bookmark; I was much less than that)
世界躺在庞大的书架上
The world settled on a great shelf
我的生命宛如凶涛间的乳白色
My life was all blank and pure white, yet surfy like the sea
瞧不见太阳是我们的父亲
Couldn’t see the sun was our Father.


「见证者」
The Witness
